Introduction

Welcome to our Cybersecurity Learning platform. Our mission is to provide clear, practical, and beginner-friendly content to help you understand digital safety, prevent cyberattacks, and build secure systems.Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting computer systems, networks, programs, and data from digital attacks, theft, or damage. It involves a combination of technologies, processes, and policies to safeguard digital assets and ensure their confidentiality, integrity, and availability. Common cyber threats include malware, phishing, and ransomware, while protective measures involve firewalls, encryption, antivirus software, and multi-factor authentication.
